About the Author


Paul Andrew Turner, J.M., M.A., M.S.

Orlando, Florida

Paul Andrew Turner is a machine learning engineer and policy analyst based in Orlando, Florida. He specializes in the intersection of advanced machine learning, macroeconomic data, and global policy.

A dedicated technologist, Andrew architects and develops machine learning models across professional and personal research. He brings a quantitative, law-schooled rigor to complex challenges, backed by a Master of Arts in Economics, a Juris Master in Financial Regulation & Compliance Law, and a Master of Science in Financial Technology.

Beyond the spreadsheet and the codebase, Andrew is a voracious reader, an active writer, and a dedicated advocate. He publishes data-driven analyses on the U.S. economy and geopolitical shifts, and actively volunteers his skills as an official event photographer for Orlando Pride. A lifelong outdoorsman with a deep commitment to community service, he has achieved the rank of Eagle Scout and is a recipient of the Vigil Honor within the Order of the Arrow. Whether engineering predictive algorithms, dissecting global economics and foreign affairs, or out on the water kayaking, Andrew brings a rigorous, data-driven approach to leaving systems fundamentally better than he found them.
